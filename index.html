World

After capitalism, the gods remained.

For two thousand years, Terra has existed without money. Communes share resources, Parenting Guilds raise children collectively, and the old nation-states are ash and myth. But when humanity overthrew capital, the gods of capital did not vanish — they fled.

Mammonas, the God of Capitalism, and Tyrannos, the God of Fascism, hid beyond the multiverse, feeding on timelines, resurrecting financial empires, and rewriting history. To keep them from returning, Terra founded a single institution: Phoenix Academy, where engineered demigods learn to kill gods.
Season 1 (Chapters 0–6) follows Aura and the Zeta-7 cohort as they navigate Phoenix Academy, commune politics, Parenting Guild rivalries, and the first hints that the pantheon of modernity is not as dead as Terra believes.
